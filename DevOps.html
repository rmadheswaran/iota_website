DevOps

DevOps is a cultural and technical movement that emphasizes collaboration, communication, and automation between software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops) teams. It aims to break down silos, streamline processes, and create a culture of continuous integration, delivery, and improvement, enabling organizations to deliver high-quality software faster and more efficiently.
